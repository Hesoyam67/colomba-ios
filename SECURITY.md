# Security Policy

## Responsible disclosure

Please report security issues privately by emailing security@colomba.app.

Do not open public GitHub issues for vulnerabilities, secrets exposure, authentication bypasses, data handling issues, or other security-sensitive findings.

## Response window

We aim to acknowledge reports within 72 hours and will follow up with triage status, remediation expectations, or clarifying questions as soon as possible.

## Scope

Security reports should focus on the Colomba iOS app, supporting app configuration, dependency risks, and any behavior that could affect customer, restaurant, caller, or operator data.

Out-of-scope items include social engineering, denial-of-service testing, spam, physical attacks, and findings that require access to accounts or systems you do not own or have explicit permission to test.
